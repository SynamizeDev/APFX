# Become a Partner — Page Structure & Messaging

**Route:** `/partners`  
**Purpose:** Attract affiliates, IBs, content creators, and business partners; explain the opportunity, benefits, and how to apply.

---

## 1. Page layout (section order)

| Section | Content |
|--------|---------|
| Hero | Title: "Become Our Partner"; short description (earn commissions by referring traders and promoting the platform); primary CTA "Apply to Become a Partner", secondary "Learn How It Works" (anchor to steps) |
| Why Partner With Us | Icon cards: Competitive commission, Reliable platform, Advanced tools, Dedicated support, Transparent tracking & payouts |
| Types of Partnerships | Four blocks: Affiliate Partner, Introducing Broker (IB), Content Creator / Influencer, Business Partner — each with short description and benefits |
| How the Partnership Works | 4 steps with icons: Apply → Get link/dashboard → Share with audience → Earn commissions |
| Commission Structure | How partners earn: commission per referred trader, revenue share from trading, tracking; include example earnings scenarios |
| Partner Tools & Resources | Dashboard, marketing materials, banners, dedicated support |
| Testimonials (optional) | Success stories or quotes from partners |
| FAQ | Who can partner, how commissions are paid, minimum payout, tracking/reporting |
| Partner Application Form | Name, Email, Website/social, Country, Partnership type; submit CTA |
| CTA | Final "Apply to Become a Partner" with anchor to form or primary action |

---

## 2. UI components

- **Benefits:** Grid of icon cards (same style as About Us values).
- **Partnership types:** Cards or two-column blocks with icon/heading, description, bullet benefits.
- **Steps:** Horizontal or vertical step strip with number/icon, title, short text (like Copy Trading "How it works").
- **Commission:** Card or table with bullet points; separate "Example earnings" card with scenario (e.g. X referred traders × Y = Z).
- **Form:** Standard inputs (text, email, select for partnership type, country); primary submit button; success/error state.
- **FAQ:** Accordion or list of Q&A pairs.

---

## 3. Conversion-focused CTA placement

- Hero: primary "Apply to Become a Partner", secondary "Learn How It Works".
- After Types or Steps: repeat "Apply Now" (anchor to form).
- End of page: "Apply to Become a Partner" again above or below form.

---

## 4. Responsive design

- Hero CTAs stack on mobile.
- Benefits and partnership types: grid → 1 col.
- Steps: horizontal → stacked on small screens.
- Form: full width, stacked fields.
- FAQ: full width.

---

## 5. Styling consistency

- Dark theme, same tokens and card style as About Us, Press, Blog.
- Reuse InnerPageHero, section/container/sectionTitle pattern, primary/secondary button styles.
